## What the pass found and fixed (commits 0479f19, 619289a, aae2298)

1. Header contrast (serious, every route): the site header tinted
   itself with the per-chapter colour behind white text (3.54 to 4.0
   against the 4.5 requirement). Fix: solid dark header, GOV.UK
   pattern; the chapter colour moved to a boundary stripe.
2. Chapter hero contrast (serious): tinted text on chapter-colour
   fills. Fix: white card, chapter-colour border, dark text; the
   kicker takes the chapter colour as text on white, which passes for
   all 17 hues.
3. Map card numbers (serious): light chapter hues as text on white
   (down to 2.7). Fix: dark text; the colour cue stays on the card
   border.
4. Breadcrumb links (serious): chapter colour on grey surface at 4.07.
   Fix: brand-dark.
5. Heading order (moderate): home feature cards and complain steps
   jumped h1 to h3. Fix: promoted to h2.
6. Landmark coverage (moderate): the Big text / Print toolbar sat
   outside all landmarks. Fix: role="region" with its existing label.
