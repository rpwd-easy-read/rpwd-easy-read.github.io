# Plain-language titles for navigation

**SIGNED OFF by Deepa 2026-07-12** after her review against the Act
text, with seven corrections applied exactly as she gave them
(S2, S8, S10, S25, S26, S31, S85). Her reasons, for the record: S10
had mentioned marriage, which Section 10 does not cover; S26 had
promised insurance to everyone when the Act limits it to government
employees with disabilities; S25's free healthcare is income tested in
the Act's own words; S31 had dropped the benchmark qualifier, and
section rows appear in search results without chapter context; S8 had
dropped armed conflict and emergencies; S2 defines terms, not
coverage; S85's prosecutor argues the state's case, not personally
the complainant's. This file is the authoritative source;
source\_build\apply_plain_titles.py writes it into content.json.

Per D-P51 decision 2. These titles appear on section rows, search
results, and chapter cards; the official title stays on the section
page under the number badge. Voice: everyday words, one idea, no
banned vocabulary. Deepa edits any row she dislikes, then signs off;
a script then writes `plain_title` into content.json. Easy Read body
text is untouched.

Observation, out of scope for this pass: a few plain_text bodies in
content.json contain en dashes (Sections 43, 46, 54, 62, 67, 69, 95,
99). The body text is locked, so left as found; flagging for a future
content pass.
